Famous Poets In The History Of India:

1. Kalidas(Shakespeare Of India)

Kalidasa was a classical Sanskrit poet and dramatist, believed to have lived during the 4th–5th century CE, possibly in the Gupta period, which is considered a golden age of Indian culture and literature. Kalidasa’s works are still studied in Indian literature and Sanskrit courses. He is often compared to William Shakespeare due to his influence and literary genius. His play Shakuntala impressed scholars in Europe, especially after being translated by Sir William Jones in the 18th century.
